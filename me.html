Welcome to my corner of the web. I created DEHUMAN because I noticed a gap between what we are told and what is actually happening. My goal is to explore ethics, government, and the common misconceptions that keep us from being truly moral individuals.

I believe that to be a "great person," one must first be willing to question the systems and narratives we've inherited. This site is a collection of my research, perspectives, and calls to action.

The Core Mission

To dismantle legalized abuse, expose institutional lies, and provide a framework for self-improvement that isn't based on corporate or political agendas.

When I'm not writing for this network, I'm usually researching animal rights, legal reform, or the philosophy of self-governance. I believe transparency is the only cure for the current state of our world.
